# Timed Clicker

**Yellow circle randomly spawns across the whole map. Your task is to as fast as you can click it. Try beating your previous best score!**

## Tiers:

**1.000**+ - bad

**1.000** - decent

**0.500** - good

**0.300** - pro

**0.176** - beated my desktop record

**0.160** - beated my mobile record

---

**This app stores your best score in local storage, so you can safely close it and come back later!**
